FAQs

Who is the Starter package for?

The Starter Package is designed for individuals and businesses seeking to establish or revamp their online presence. It caters to those who either do not have a website yet or have a small existing website with up to three pages that requires a fresh new look and improved functionality. Whether you're a startup, a freelancer, a small business, or someone looking to kickstart your online journey, the Starter Package provides the perfect solution to get you started on the path to a stunning and effective website presence.

Who is the Professional package for?

The Professional Package is specifically designed for established businesses seeking a comprehensive website redesign to elevate their existing online presence. It caters to those who recognize the importance of a refreshed website and aim to upgrade their web presence for maximum impact. If your business already has an online presence but needs a significant revamp to stay competitive, the Professional Package is the perfect solution. Our team of experts will work closely with you to transform your current website through a strategic website redesign, ensuring it aligns seamlessly with your brand, attracts your target audience, and drives tangible results. With a focus on delivering a visually stunning and high-performing website, the Professional Package ensures your business remains at the forefront of the digital landscape.

Who is the Advance package for?

The Advanced Package is ideal for businesses and organizations with larger websites, spanning up to 20 pages, who are looking for a comprehensive website upgrade coupled with powerful SEO optimization. If you have an extensive online presence with multiple pages and content to showcase, the Advanced Package is the perfect fit. Our expert team will not only revamp and optimize your website for a visually stunning and user-friendly experience but also implement advanced SEO strategies to boost your online visibility and search engine rankings. Whether you operate an e-commerce platform, a content-rich website, or a complex web application, the Advanced Package ensures your website is primed for success in the digital landscape
