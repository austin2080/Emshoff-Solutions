Wix Migration To Shopify

Project Overview

Monique was looking for a developer to redesign her current store and suggestions for a new e-Commerce platform. I recommended that she should migrate over to Shopify from Wix. I walked her through the process of switching platforms and gave her peace of mind that her current SEO progress wouldn't be affected.

One of the biggest hurdles Monique facing with selling second hand luxury bags like Gucci and Luis Vuitton was her current website was lacking trust. Luxury items are constantly getting knocked off and we needed to overcome those concerns and that's exactly what we did by: 

  • Explaining how she guaranteed the authenticity of each and every bag
  • Providing high-quality images of each product
  • Featuring herself and her family on her About us page

Once the website was ready to launch, I recorded personalized training videos for Monique since she was new to Shopify.
This project took a little over two weeks to complete but we haven't stopped working together! Since the launch, I have continued to support Monique either through new projects or being there for her to bounce ideas off of.

If you're looking for a like new handbag, definitely check out the collection at OC Luxury Bags and her Instagram account as well!
