Shopify Store Setup

Project Overview

Ashley was new to e-Commerce and chose to get started with Go Daddy but she was getting frustrated like many beginners do. She came to me looking for help and guidance. She didn't make very much progress with Go Daddy so I recommended that she get started with Shopify (Yes, I love Shopify!!). The project was a full store design & setup on Shopify but I also included additional training about how to use the platform.

This project was a pretty straightforward Shopify store setup but Ashley did request a few customizations that were outside of her theme capabilities. Here a few highlights of the project:

  • Custom product description metafields
  • Figma mockups to fully developed Shopify store
  • Personlized training videos to limit the amount of development requests which ultimately saves money
